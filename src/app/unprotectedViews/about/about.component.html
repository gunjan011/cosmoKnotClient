About  


Cosmoknot was founded in 2018 with the purpose of connecting educational resources with aspiring astronauts. Partnering with tech giants like Amazon and SpaceX, Cosmoknot seeks to reinvent and streamline the training process and connect prospective students with instructors. As an extension of NASA, we encourage any prospective applicants to fill out the form below and submit their credentials to our administrative staff. Upon being approved for enrollment, you will have access to your own Cosmoknot student portal that will allow you to post journal entries and view assignments.
